# User Guide

## Getting Started

1. Visit the IndraNet marketplace
2. Browse available hosts (filter by GPU, price, availability)
3. Click a host to see their specs and reviews
4. Choose session duration
5. Complete payment via Stripe Checkout
6. Connect — your session starts automatically

## During a Session

- **Mouse:** Click anywhere on the stream to lock your cursor. Press `Escape` to unlock.
- **Keyboard:** All keyboard input is forwarded when cursor is locked.
- **Gamepad:** Plug in an Xbox controller — it's detected automatically.
- **Audio:** System audio from the host streams to your speakers.

## Ending a Session

Click "End Session" in the top-right corner. Sessions also end automatically when your balance runs out (you'll see a warning at 5 minutes remaining).

## Billing

Sessions are billed per minute at the host's listed hourly rate. Your wallet balance is shown live during the session. You can add more funds from your dashboard at any time.

## Session Quality

If the stream looks choppy, try:
- Switching to a wired ethernet connection
- Reducing browser tab count
- Choosing a host geographically closer to you

## Privacy

Sessions are isolated: the host cannot see your activity, and you cannot access the host's files. All session state is destroyed when the session ends.
