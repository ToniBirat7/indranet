# Vast.ai Analysis

## What They Do
Headless GPU marketplace. Renters get SSH/Jupyter access to bare metal or container instances. No GUI, no streaming.

## Strengths
- Very low prices ($0.10-0.80/hr for gaming GPUs)
- Large supply of hosts (crypto miners diversifying)
- Per-second billing

## Weaknesses
- No interactive GUI (you can't run a game)
- No sandboxing — users get raw shell access (security risk for hosts)
- CLI-heavy (not consumer-friendly)
- No streaming infrastructure

## Relevance to IndraNet
Vast.ai proves the host supply side: people will rent their GPUs. But they left the interactive use case completely unaddressed. Our Phase 1 MVP addresses their biggest gap.
