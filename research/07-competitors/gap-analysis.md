## Our Moat

1. **Open marketplace + interactive streaming combined:** No one else does this.
2. **Idle hardware monetization:** Gaming PCs idle 20+ hours/day. We unlock that value.
3. **Host-set pricing:** Market rates rather than fixed tiers.
4. **P2P architecture:** Lower infrastructure cost than centralized providers.
