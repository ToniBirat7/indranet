# RunPod Analysis

## What They Do
GPU cloud marketplace focused on AI/ML workloads. Docker container-based. Jupyter notebooks and SSH access.

## Strengths
- Good GPU availability (RTX 3090, A100, H100)
- Reasonable pricing ($0.40-2.00/hr)
- Community cloud (peer hardware) + secure cloud (data center)

## Weaknesses
- No interactive GUI streaming
- Containers only (no VM, no full OS)
- CUDA-focused — not designed for gaming

## Relevance to IndraNet
RunPod proves AI/ML developers will pay for GPU compute on peer hardware. Phase 2+ opportunity: IndraNet could serve the AI/ML market once we have DDA passthrough.
