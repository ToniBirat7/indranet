## Our Positioning
IndraNet targets the gap between:
- Headless GPU clouds (too technical, no GUI)
- Cloud gaming services (centralized, no open hardware marketplace)

**Target price range:** $0.50 - $3.00/hr depending on GPU tier.
- Budget tier (RTX 3070): $0.50-0.80/hr
- Mid tier (RTX 3090/4080): $1.00-1.50/hr  
- High tier (RTX 4090): $1.50-2.50/hr

Hosts set their own price within a platform-defined range.
